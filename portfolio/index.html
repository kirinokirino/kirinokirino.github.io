Japanese tools

I am learning languages as a hobby. When studying japanese, I had a couple of ideas for tools that would be nice to have by my side.
I used scripts in this repo to crawl through jisho.org dictionary, to make a custom anki-deck (structuring relations between words). I also used the data from it in my Twitch chat app, to generate transcriptions for japanese words.

Space delivery

Its awesome when bits and pieces come together and work and you get to see a finished project! This is a game for WASM-4: a virtual retro console for compiled langugaes.
Its api is very limited: read some bits to get the input, a couple of functions to draw and play a sound. I used knowledge from other projects to implement physics, procedural space, used dithering for drawing, implemented my own convenience math operation, parallax for background planets. At the last day, I implemented music system: It uses scales and predefined patterns to make procedural music. It needed more experimentation and polish to tweak the parameters though. It was fun!
